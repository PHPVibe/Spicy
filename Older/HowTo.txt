Spicy, the new xClub, updated for PHPVibe 5.
Moves from cUrl, adds an ajax loader for tags and pornstars in the Redtube importer's form. 
To install simply upload the folders /moderator and /plugins to your server. 
Head in Admin to Settings -> Plugins and enable the plugin. 
Refresh the page and check the new menus in "Add Media". 

Enjoy using and customizing this!
